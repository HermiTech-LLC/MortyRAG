# The Mysteries of Black Holes: Fact vs. Fiction

**Separating the Science from the Sci-Fi**

Black holes have long fascinated both scientists and storytellers. These mysterious cosmic entities are the ultimate playground for blending fact with fiction. In this document, we'll explore what science tells us about black holes and how popular culture has expanded upon these truths with imaginative fiction.

## The Science: What We Know

Black holes are regions of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape. They are formed from the remnants of massive stars that collapse under their own gravity. The boundaries of a black hole, known as the event horizon, represent the point of no return.
