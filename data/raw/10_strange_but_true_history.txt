## The Dancing Plague of 1518

In the summer of 1518, residents of Strasbourg, France, were struck by a strange compulsion to dance uncontrollably. What started with a single woman soon spread to dozens, then hundreds, who danced for days on end without rest. The cause remains a mystery to this day.
